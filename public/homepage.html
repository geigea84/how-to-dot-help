HowTo.Help

HowTo.Help is a nonpartisan project aiming to bridge the gap between caring and acting. Become a volunteer today!

about

With the coronavirus pandemic's mounting death toll and nationwide protests against police violence, many Americans find themselves asking, "How can I help?" Urgent needs are everywhere we look, even as we each struggle with our own hardships. HowTo.Help will help Americans who want to get more involved in their communities and turn compassion into action.
